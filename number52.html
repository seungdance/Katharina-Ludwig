NUMBER 52
SOLO
2025/2026
15 / 30 / 60 min

A solo about dance, expectation and the art of remaining true to oneself.

A unique mix between dance and stand-up comedy.

A humorous insight into the daily lives of dancers.

With wit, self-irony and precise movement, NUMBER 52 looks at the often absurd aspects of the dance world: auditions, project work, idealised demands - and the constant balancing act between adaptation and authenticity.

The performer takes the audience on a personal, humorous journey through her experiences as a contemporary dancer.

Inspired by stand-up comedy, the stage space becomes a narrative space - full of movement, reflection and surprising twists and turns.

But despite all the humour, NUMBER 52 is also a piece about resilience: about the strength to keep reinventing yourself, to keep going and not to lose the joy of movement. A tribute to the versatility and resilience of dancers - and to dance itself.

Price Awarded Dancer 2025

at SoloDuo Competition, Barnes Crossing Cologne, May 2025

Laudatio of the Jury Katharina shows great versatility as a dancer and performer. She combines different dance styles ranging from ballet to breaking, displays great qualities as a stand-up comedian and handles text well. The jury applauds her ability to deliver a strong critique towards the dance field as an industry while keeping the performance light-hearted and witty.
