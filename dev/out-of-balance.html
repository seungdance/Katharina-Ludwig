OUT OF BALANCE

Year

2025

Format

Ensemble Performance

Performers

3 Dancers

Description

A dynamic ensemble piece exploring themes of equilibrium and disruption.

Three performers navigate the delicate balance between stability and chaos, using white boxes as both props and metaphors for the structures we build and break in our lives.

The work examines how we maintain our center while the world around us shifts and changes, creating a powerful visual metaphor for contemporary existence.
