OUT OF BALANCE

Trio (14 min)
A play of illusion with gravity

Concept

Gravity functions as our orientation point in the room, shaping our physicality and forming a visual and proprioceptive reception.

But what would happen if gravity shifts its force? Could we climb up walls, hang from the ceiling or float feet upside down?

How would we react to a sudden throw out of balance that affects the whole world around us? A research about imbalance, force and proprioception.
