INFERO

Year

2025

Venue

ZANT Festival

Format

Outdoor Performance

Description

A dynamic outdoor performance piece created for the ZANT Festival.

INFERO explores themes of transformation and renewal, using the natural outdoor setting as both backdrop and collaborator in the choreography.

The work examines the relationship between performer and environment, creating a powerful dialogue between human movement and the surrounding landscape.
