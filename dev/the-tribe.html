THE TRIBE

Year

2025

Format

Ensemble Performance

Performers

5 Dancers

Description

A powerful ensemble piece exploring themes of unity, identity, and collective movement.

Five dancers, clad in identical light-colored suits with headlamps, create a mysterious and futuristic atmosphere that examines the dynamics of group behavior and individual expression within a collective.

The work investigates how individuals maintain their identity while being part of a larger group, using synchronized yet individualistic movements to create a compelling narrative about belonging and autonomy.
