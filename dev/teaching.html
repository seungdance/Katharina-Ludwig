Teaching

Katharina is an educated dance pedagogue who regularly teaches hobby and vocational classes as well as training for professional dancers. She teaches classes that are specifically tailored to the demands of Contemporary Dancers today. Her teaching repertory includes Contemporary, Improvisation, Classical Ballet, Jazz, as well as Fitness and Mobility Classes. In October 2023 Katharina completed the Pretraining of the GYROTONIC© method.

Studios that Katharina taught at include tanzplatz Vorbern, akar, ballsaal, Tanzschule Kreutzberg, tanzszene Bern, Unisport Bern, Conservatoire de Fribourg, Next Step Freiburg, Studio Pro Arte and more…

Her teaching experience for professional dancers includes teaching at Tanzhaus Zurich, Tanzbüro Basel, TANZhAUS Bern, Tanznetz Freiburg as well as for various professional dance companies.

Katharina's contemporary dance training combines fixed movement sequences with improvisation exercises based on contemporary technique and floorwork. After a warm-up that builds the dancers' strength and endurance, the training progresses to floor work. Afterwards, the dancers move through the space, standing or on the floor, with sequences that combine flowing movements with powerful, dynamic elements. In addition to its physicality, the class is based on play, groove and the joy of movement. An emphasis lies on establishing a connection between dance and music. Through different rhythmic exercises the training playfully expands the dancer's own movement vocabulary. The class offers a variety of dance material that moves the body as a whole and encourages the dancers to deepen both their technical skills as well as their artistic expressiveness.
