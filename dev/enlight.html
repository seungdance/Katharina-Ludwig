ENLIGHT

Year

2025

Format

Contemporary Dance

Technique

Long Exposure Photography

Description

An experimental dance piece that explores the intersection of movement and light.

Using long exposure photography techniques, ENLIGHT captures the ephemeral nature of dance, transforming performers into luminous streaks of energy.

The work examines how light can reveal the hidden patterns and rhythms of human movement, creating a mesmerizing visual experience that blurs the line between performance and photography.
