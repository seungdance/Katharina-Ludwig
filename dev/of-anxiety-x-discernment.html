OF ANXIETY X DISCERNMENT

Year

2025

Status

Work in Progress

Format

Contemporary Dance

Description

A contemporary dance piece exploring the intersection of anxiety and discernment in modern life.

This work delves into the complex emotional landscape where uncertainty meets clarity, examining how we navigate decisions and judgments in an increasingly complex world.

Through movement, the piece investigates the tension between internal turmoil and external composure, creating a visceral experience of the human condition.
