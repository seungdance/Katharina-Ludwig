OF ANXIETY
X
DISCERNMENT
SOLO
13 min
2022

Developed and performed at Performing Mondays im delphi Space Freiburg. Further shows at Summer Stage Theater Freiburg (Germany) and in the graduating program of Anton-Bruckner Universität Linz.

This solo is an exploration on fear and the bodily experience of it, influenced by Philipp's album, which explores this theme musically.

Is the young generation stuck in crisis after crisis? What does this do to this generation, to the young people growing up in our society? How do we deal with fear and this certain feeling of helplessness? And can we find power in change through our situation?
