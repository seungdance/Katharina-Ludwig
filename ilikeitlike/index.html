I LIKE IT LIKE...
GROUP PIECE (10 dancers)
20 min
Premiere: 15. December 2025, Teatro Greco, Rome (IT)

Dress up, feel good, show off, throw away, buy new. I LIKE IT LIKE… humorously explores the contradiction within our clothes. While we love to slip into different versions of ourselves, fast fashion fuels global and ecological inequality. The piece plays with this tension and asks: why do we still want more?
